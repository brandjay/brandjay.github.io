Cybersecurity Tips
  • Use a secure web broswer. A few good ones are Microsoft edge, Google chrome, and the Brave browser.
  • Be careful about visiting sketchy websites. This can range from free movie websites to torrenting.
  • Be cautious of apps you download and files you open. "THINK BEFORE YOU OPEN OR CLICK".
  • Update your computer regularly. This may get annoying, but it's done for your safety.
